About NexBus

System Overview

The NexBus Bus Ticket Booking System is designed to streamline bus ticket booking for customers while providing bus operators with efficient tools to manage their fleet. It offers real-time information on bus schedules, seat availability, and bookings, ensuring a seamless experience for all users.

Bus Operator Role

As a Bus Operator, you are responsible for managing your bus services, including:

  • Buses: Add, update, or remove buses, assign routes and amenities, and view associated schedules.
  • Drivers: Manage driver information and assign them to buses.
  • Seats: Configure seat details and update availability in real-time.
  • Routes: View available routes and assign them to your buses.
  • Schedules: Create and manage multiple departure schedules for your buses, including fares and timings.
  • Maintenance: Record and track maintenance activities for your fleet.
  • Bookings: Monitor and cancel customer bookings as needed.

Use the dashboard to access these features and keep your services running smoothly.
